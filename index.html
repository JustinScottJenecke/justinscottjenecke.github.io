About Me

More about what I do, my experience, and my plans

Tutoring and Training

I do freelance tutoring in coding and programming languages, and I primarily offer my services to University students and High School students, since I have formal experience tutoring in this demographic.
Tutoring typically involves assisting students with projects and assignments based on their own curriculum. However, I also conduct sessions where I teach concepts using isolated examples or create build-along projects with students to help solidify coding concepts.

Upskilling in Development:

With regards to backend skills, I have recently picked up Spring-Boot again, I am also learning ASP .NET Core and C#, starting studying towards and AWS cerfication to learn more about the Cloud.
In the frontend space, I have recently completed a TypeScript certification on Scrimba and I in the process of migrating this websites dynamic content and functionality to TypeScript, and I am also doing a second Scrimba course in Advanced React, to improve my React skills and learn more custom hooks, advanced routing, and performance in the browser.

Education and experience:

I started my coding journey developing desktop applications and REST APIs in Java, whilst at University at CPUT, and have expanded my skillset to Full-Stack development by building customer facing applications, using tools such as JavaScript and React.js, and having those clients consume data and services hosted by the backend practice projects I have built.
My professional experience is around 3 years, with the bulk of my experience in the industry revolving around training and mentoring students learning to code for the first time, with rest of my experience relating to development internships, and adminstrative work.

Future Plans:

My current goal is to explore more technologies relating to software architecture and learning more about distributed systems. I have also been getting back into the .Net framework, and learning more about databases, and I have also been doubling down on learning React, and am currently working on completing a TypeScript course on Scrimba.
