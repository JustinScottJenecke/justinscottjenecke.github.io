Tutoring

Get assistance with coding homework and assignments, build micro programs to improve understanding of coding concepts, and more. All available in a 1-on-1, or a group setting.

Services on offer:

A bit more details on how my tutoring services work,
what I offer, and why students are my primary demographic.

Tutoring Service

Tutoring and mentoring for students typically consists of online sessions where I assist students with any array of coding-related issues. Means include assisting with school/uni assignments, Q/A discussions, teaching new concepts and creating isolated examples to explain them, etc. Students are welcome to send their lesson plans as well, as this will help keep tutoring sessions coherent with topics of a student's school curriculum.

Build Along Projects

I also offer build-along projects for those who pefer to keep School/Uni work seperate from tutoring sessions. Build-along sessions are sessions where my screen is shared and I code along with you, I explain concepts, and I explain my thought process, as I am building out the project. This doubles as learning and practice experience, since you as the student are required to code the projects as well using recordngs made during our sessions.

How topics for sessions are selected

With standard tutoring sessions, the topic for discussion or focul point of the coding excercises are usually selected by the student(s) before sessions. With group sessions, studets can vote for a topic from a list of all topics/concepts, and the topic with the most votes for it will be the one chose for the session. Multiple topics can be discussed in a single session given the urgency of the student wanting assistance, time constraints, and the approval of the other students in the session.

Tutoring Models and offerings

Offerings include 1-on-1 sessions, with the standard session being 1 hour long, and the price being per hour. Pricing is outlined in the pricing section. This option is good for those seeking more individual attention or have a very specific problem that need a deep debugging session to resolve.

I also offer group sessions which function as a more cost-effective option and run for a bit longer at 90 minutes, to make up for possible lack of individual attention due to number of attendees. This model is great for learning to work in a team, networking with other novice devs like yourself, and gaining exposure to issues and bugs others struggling from and to hopefully learn from them.

Group Sessions

Minimum number of students per group are 4 in order to ensure that the sessions are still cost effective to host.

Sessions also have a maximum number capped at 6, in order to ensure all students get the most of the time allocated to the session.

At this point in time, the models offered are unfortunately all online as there are too many logistical issues involved with in-person tutoring and training, however this might be subject to change in the future.
